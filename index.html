ABOUT ROAD TRIP ADVISOR

Key features of our Web Application

Responsive

It will help people plan amazing road trips. After entering a starting and destination point, the web application will suggest a couple of road trip alternatives overlayed with different restaurant, hotels and activities options optimized with user preferences.

Passion

Each road trip will be tailored to perfectly fit you and your family. Using latest machine learning algorithms, we will base your trip itinerary using information you feed into our application in regards to your preferences for meals, events, activities, how often you need bathroom breaks, how many poeple you are road trippin' with and even what time you need to be in bed by!

Design

We aim to give our users the best user experience along side with a fun and interactive UI
